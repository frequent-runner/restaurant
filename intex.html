Pezzo Perfetto Italian Restaurant Kochi

AUTHENTIC ITALIAN RESTAURANT IN KOCHI

Welcome to Pezza Perfetto Italian Restaurant. We’re a family-run Italian Restaurant located in kochi, close to fort kochi beach. We offer a wide range of authentic Italian dishes, a warm welcome and a great atmosphere.

We pride ourselves on the quality of our food. Each dish is carefully crafted by our Sicilian chef who brings over 20 years’ experience to the table. We use high quality, fresh ingredients in all of our dishes, including seasonal produce to ensure our customers have the very best dining experience.

At Pezza Perfetto Italian restaurant, we like to keep life interesting with exciting events, bespoke menus and promotions every month.
